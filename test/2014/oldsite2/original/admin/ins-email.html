This function allows you to change or remove the bounce/reply-to mail address for the administrator or your user. The bounce or reply-to email for the administrator or your users will be sent to this new address.

[Overview] [Fields] [Notes] [Samples] [Errors]
User Name: (Required)The Username whose bounce/reply-to address will be changed/removed, you can select from a list.

Bounce/Reply-to:(Required)New Bounce/Reply-to address for your selected username.

[Overview] [Fields] [Notes] [Samples] [Errors]
When you change the username, the old bounce/reply-to email address for this user will be displayed in the Bounce/Reply-to filed.
