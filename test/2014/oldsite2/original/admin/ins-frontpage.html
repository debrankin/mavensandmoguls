[Overview] [Fields] [Notes] [Samples] [Errors]
If you receive an error from FrontPage regarding your extensions, try removing them and then reinstalling them.

If your links are not working properly or images are not displaying after publishing, you may recalculate hyper links by opening the web in FrontPage, clicking Tools, then Recalculate Hyper links.

If your site is large, it may take some time (5+ minutes) for the processes to run.
